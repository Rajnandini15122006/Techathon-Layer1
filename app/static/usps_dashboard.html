Spatial Variation Model
Each grid cell has unique conditions based on location. Urban core areas (within 5km of Pune center) have higher traffic but better drainage infrastructure. Peripheral areas have lower traffic but older drainage systems. Rainfall varies ±30% across the region.
SCS-CN Hydrological Model
Using Soil Conservation Service Curve Number method for runoff estimation. Curve numbers vary by land use: Built-up (92.5), Residential (80), Vegetation (62.5). Deterministic and audit-ready calculations.
USPS Formula
USPS = 0.4 × Rain Index + 0.4 × Drain Stress + 0.2 × Traffic Index
Multi-criteria composite scoring with transparent weighted aggregation. Each cell computed independently.
